What I Do

  • 01

    3D Designs

    Creating detailed and realistic 3D models and visualizations that bring landscape concepts to life...

    Read More

    My 3D design work focuses on bringing landscape ideas to life through precise modeling and realistic visualization. I use SketchUp to build accurate and detailed 3D models that reflect the spatial qualities and material selections of each outdoor space.

    For photorealistic renderings, I rely on Lumion, which enables me to produce immersive visuals with natural lighting, textures, and environmental effects—ideal for presenting final concepts to clients. In combination with AutoCAD, I ensure that every 3D model is based on accurate technical drawings, ensuring consistency between planning and visualization.

  • 02

    Landscape Designs

    I design outdoor spaces that are both beautiful and practical, creating places where nature and people can connect. Each project is carefully planned to suit its specific location and meet the needs of the people who will use it....

    Read More

    My landscape design process goes far beyond layout and aesthetics—it’s about creating a fully integrated outdoor environment. I begin by analyzing the site context and project requirements to develop a design that balances beauty, functionality, and sustainability..

    A core part of my work involves selecting suitable plant species based on climate, soil type, and maintenance needs. I design with a diverse palette that includes trees, shrubs, groundcovers, ornamental grasses, flowering plants, and seasonal flowers, ensuring year-round visual interest and ecological value..

  • 03

    Irrigation System Design

    Designing efficient and modern irrigation networks using high-quality systems from trusted brands like Hunter and Rain Bird. I create tailored solutions that ensure every plant gets the right amount of water with minimal waste....

    Read More

    I design smart and reliable irrigation systems using the latest technology from Hunter and Rain Bird, including modern sprinklers, drip lines, and automated controllers. Each system is planned based on the site layout, plant types, and water availability, ensuring effective coverage and water conservation. My goal is to keep landscapes healthy and green while reducing maintenance effort and water use. Whether for residential gardens or larger projects, the systems I design are easy to manage, long-lasting, and adapted to the specific needs of the space..
